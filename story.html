The Misty Story

A premium high-range sanctuary born from a simple dream: to merge architectural genius with ecological preservation.

Rooted in Earth

We are Misty Canopy, a sanctuary of stillness. Twelve acres of untamed, high-altitude Western Ghats forest where the ancient wilderness was not cleared or tamed, but deeply honored and integrated.

More than a resort — a philosophy

Coexisting with the Forest

Misty Canopy was founded on a commitment to eco-luxury. Every single elevated treehouse support was raised without damaging a single ancient root or cutting a single timber branch.

Our glass chalets are 100% self-powered, utilizing silent solar grids and mountain-stream hydro-turbines. Water is harvested directly from pristine highland springs, micro-filtered, and returned pure to the ecosystem.

Our cardamom and organic tea fields are maintained by local tribal families, ensuring that the heritage of Idukki district is not just preserved, but shared with deep respect and mutual prosperity.
